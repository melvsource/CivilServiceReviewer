15 Minutes

Learning Objective

Understand vocabulary through context clues, prefixes, suffixes, root words, synonyms and antonyms.

Introduction

Vocabulary questions appear in almost every Civil Service Examination. They measure whether you understand words as they are naturally used in sentences—not merely whether you have memorized dictionary definitions.

Exam Tip

Always try to predict the meaning before looking at the choices.

Worked Example

The supervisor was known for being diligent in checking reports.


What does diligent mean?


A. Lazy

B. Hardworking

C. Forgetful

D. Careless

Correct Answer: B. Hardworking
