Vocabulary

Estimated Time: 15 Minutes

Difficulty: ⭐⭐☆☆☆


Learning Objectives

  • Understand commonly tested vocabulary.
  • Use context clues.
  • Recognize synonyms.
  • Recognize antonyms.
  • Eliminate incorrect answers quickly.

Introduction

Vocabulary questions measure how well you understand the meaning of words as they are used in context. Many questions can be answered correctly without memorizing a dictionary because the surrounding sentence provides valuable clues.


Example

The supervisor was known for being diligent in checking reports before submission.


What does diligent most nearly mean?


A. Lazy

B. Hardworking

C. Forgetful

D. Careless


Correct Answer: B. Hardworking


Exam Tip

Read the sentence first. Try replacing the word with your own simpler word before looking at the choices.
