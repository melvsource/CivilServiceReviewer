LESSON 1

Vocabulary

Estimated Study Time: 15 Minutes

Difficulty: ⭐⭐☆☆☆


Learning Objective

By the end of this lesson, you should be able to determine the meaning of unfamiliar words using context clues, synonyms, antonyms, prefixes, suffixes, and root words.


Introduction

Vocabulary questions measure how well you understand English words as they are used in sentences. The Civil Service Examination often presents a word or phrase and asks you to choose the closest meaning.


Example

The manager was known for being diligent in checking every report before submission.


What does diligent mean?


A. Lazy

B. Careful and hardworking

C. Careless

D. Forgetful


Answer: B. Careful and hardworking.


Exam Tip

Don't memorize thousands of words. Instead, learn how words are used inside sentences. Context is often enough to determine the correct answer.
